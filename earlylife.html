Robert's Early Life

Return to Introduction Page

Robert Downey Jr. was born on April 4th, 1965 in Manhattan, New York. He was born along with his sister Allyson who was born in 1963. Their father was a filmaker who created many movies in the late 60s and into the 70s. Robert, his sister and his mother all starred in his fathers movies. Robert attended highschool in California for a few years before dropping out and returning to NYC to continue his career in acting. He starred in a few more movies then from time to time got in trouble with the law, at the time Robert was also addicted to drugs. In 1999 he was sentanced to 3 years in prison but was able to get out early. In 2000 robert had a role in a show that got him a golden globe award. Getting this award was the beginning of Robert turning his life around and becoming a true inspiration to all. from here robert continued to do great in acting and becoming a better person and it would only continue.
